We have been given the opportunity to interview an anonymous recovering drug addict, here are some of the responses to questions we asked.

Interview Question/Answer

Question: What drug are you addicted to?
Answer: Kratom
Question: What is it?
Answer: It is an herb usually found growing in tropical climates such as Southeast Asia and Indochina
Question: So how did you abuse the herb?
Answer: I took it at high dosages which made me not only very sleepy but also caused me to have hallucinations while under the influence and was always so confused when it all ended.
Question: What were some of the effects?
Answer: I became addicted to the drug and thought it more as a coping mechanism rather than abuse. I suffered with anorexia and extreme weight loss. My family soon come to view me as someone who couldn’t take care of himself. And my kids saw me and told teachers and friends that their daddy was dreaming and shouting and wouldn’t want to eat with the family. They soon took my kids away from us and my wife filed for a divorce shortly after. I lost everything I knew.
Question: How did you overcome your addiction?
Answer: Lots of therapy. But not only did that help, I started going to church again and renewed my faith in God. Only through constant prayer and faith was I able to make it to where I am today. I have been clean for four years, and even though my life is in shambles I know that by staying away from addiction I can slowly but surely piece it all back together.
