Ever wander how cocaine affects the body? Here is an informational video depicting how cocaine/crack cocaine can negatively affect your body psychology. Cocaine travels through the lungs into your blood and eventually all the way to your brain. After reaching your brain the specific molecule has a chemical structure(or at least a portion of it) similar to that of dopamine(a sympathetic neurotransmitter) which binds with the reward receptors of your brain giving users the high that they often feel. Unfortunately after the high wears off the user exhibits emotions of depression which will motivate him/her to use more cocaine. This is a vicious cycle which can quickly degrade the quality of life for the user.
